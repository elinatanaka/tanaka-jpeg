dress up games!

for some reason, I have had a lifelong obsession with dress up games. In childhood, I learned how to download web pages so that I could have an offline dress up game always at my disposal. Dialup was brutal. They were always simple click-and-drag flash games, sometimes with rows of assets that would have me scrolling past dozens of rows of clothing just for a pixellated flared jean.
In high school, I learned how to make my own click and drag flash dressup. With the death of flash, I am currently in the process of recreating this with Animate CC, html5, and JS. I know no JS so this should be a long and educational project.
